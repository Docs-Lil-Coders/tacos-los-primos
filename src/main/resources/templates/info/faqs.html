FAQS

What type of cuisine do you serve at your Mexican restaurant?

We specialize in authentic Mexican cuisine, offering a diverse menu with traditional dishes like tacos, enchiladas, tortas, and more.

Can I make a reservation in advance?

Absolutely! We encourage making reservations, especially during peak hours, to ensure you have a table waiting for you.

Are you kid-friendly?

Yes, we welcome families and have a kid's menu with tasty options that are sure to please our young guests.

Can you accommodate large groups or parties?

Of course! We can accommodate large groups for special events or celebrations. Please contact us in advance to make arrangements.

Do you offer catering services for events?

Yes, we provide catering services for various events, such as weddings, corporate gatherings, and private parties. Our catering menu offers a variety of options to suit your needs.

Can I place a takeout order?

Absolutely! We offer takeout services, and you can either call ahead or order online through our website.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash, major credit cards, and mobile payment options for your convenience.

Are your salsas and guacamole made in-house?

Yes, all our salsas and guacamole are freshly made in-house with authentic Mexican ingredients for the best taste.

Is your restaurant accessible for individuals with disabilities?

Yes, our restaurant is fully accessible, and we strive to provide a comfortable dining experience for all our guests.
