ABOUT US

Welcome to Tacos Los Primos, where the authentic flavors of Mexico come together to create a fiesta for your taste buds. We take immense pride in serving the most delectable tacos and Mexican dishes, made with love and care. Our mission is to share the vibrant spirit of Mexico through our culinary delights and warm hospitality. Join us for an unforgettable dining experience filled with mouthwatering flavors and a festive ambiance that celebrates the essence of Mexico.

OUR STORY

Tacos Los Primos is the dream of three cousins, Juan, Miguel, and Carlos, who grew up in Mexico surrounded by the rich flavors of their abuela's recipes. When they moved to the United States, they decided to share their passion for authentic Mexican cuisine with their community. With dedication and love, they opened Tacos Los Primos, where they serve up the taste of their cherished hometown and a celebration of family tradition in every dish.
